Seth Godin on Not Reinventing the Wheel

  • Share on Twitter  
  • Follow @twitortat

Steal your business model. We don’t have a shortage of business models, it’s okay if you pick one that’s already working for someone else. When it comes down to the thing you will be known for, your uniqueness, your gift, your thing worth talking about–don’t steal that. For the rest, honor those that came before and use their work as a building block for yours.

Seth Godin

A succinct gem by Seth Godin on why it’s time we stopped reinventing the wheel. See original article.
