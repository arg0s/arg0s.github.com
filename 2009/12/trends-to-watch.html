    Trends to Watch

    Our team at Levitum has put together a list of key mobile cloud trends to watch in 2010 based on market research. While some of this is stating the obvious, as a whole, I think is extremely insightful and relevant in this rapidly changing landscape.

    In short:
    • Apps will move to the cloud
    • App Stores will thrive
    • Battle for the cloud has begun
    • Open standards and technologies will succeed
    • Mobile cloud based services will emerge
    • Enterprises will embrace the cloud
